    How the score works

    Each ticker is evaluated on six factors. Scores range from 0 to 100; a stock above the threshold is rated Sustainable.

    FactorMax ptsWhat it measures
    Payout Ratio20Fraction of earnings paid as dividends; lower is safer
    Dividend Yield15Sweet spot 3–5%; penalises yield traps above 7%
    Debt / Equity15Financial leverage; lower means more room to keep paying
    Return on Equity15Profitability of the business
    Current Ratio10Short-term liquidity
    Free Cash Flow10Positive FCF is the minimum bar
    5-Year Div CAGR15Dividend growth over the past five years

    Data is fetched live from Yahoo Finance via yfinance. Results depend on the completeness of Yahoo's reported figures.
